Marine/Cargo Survey

We are committed in providing quality marine cargo surveying services to a wide variety of clients in the Maritime Industry. We will treat all clients and employees with the utmost respect and dignity. We will ensure our clients’ needs are satisfied in a timely, professional and unbiased manner.

Pre-vetting

We understand a good ranking in the OCIMF/SIRE Database is an essential tool for independent owners of tanker vessels in order to ensure employment for their vessels. It also helps in the commercial market value of the vessel in her trading patterns. A Pre-vetting Inspection can help in minimizing or even eliminating the risk of negative observations during a SIRE vetting, thus maintaining a low observation score in the database.

     Pre-Purchase           Inspections

We understand the need to keep our client promptly informed with detailed reports prior taking the responsibility of a vessel for whatever the usage maybe. Vessel and machinery are surveyed and a detailed report issued to a potential buyer for a specific vessel.We can provide sound advice on value, condition and repair cost and duration estimates to our client as the case may dictate.

Pre-loading Advisory Surveys

We offer Pre-shipment / Loss Prevention Surveys for Bulk Cargoes such as Rice Sugar, Tapioca, Alumina, Grain etc.

           General Condition      Surveys

Conducting On-hire and Off-hire Condition on Charterer’s and Owner’s behalf.

     Flag State Inspection    (FSI)

Flag State Inspections are carried out by approved Flag State Inspectors and include verification of statutory documentation and a general examination of the vessel's structure, machinery and equipment as well as a more thorough inspection and/or operational testing of fire-fighting equipment, life-saving appliances and safety equipment. We are authorised by Antigua & Bermuda flag to conduct inspection on their behalf.

On / Off Hire Bunker Surveys

In order to avoid disputes, we offer bunker quantity surveys at the time of On-Hire, Off- Hire and supply of bunker to vessels.

           Tally / Hatch            Sealing

We offer the services of Tally Checking and Hatch Sealing.

Loading, Discharging Supervision

We offer continuous Loading / Discharge supervision on board for various cargoes.

       Containerised             Cargo
